Golden retriever

Golden Retriever is the fifth-most popular dog breed by registrations (92,994) worldwide.

  • Breed Group: Gundog
  • Length of coat: Medium
  • Size: Large
  • Lifespan: Over 10 Years

The first Lord Tweedmouth is credited as the founder of the breed with a yellow dog bred from black Wavy Coated Retrievers crossed with the Tweed Water Spaniel. This mating produced four yellow puppies.
